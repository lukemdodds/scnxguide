  • Spotter Search:




    Details:

    This policy checks how quickly a process that spawned werfault.exe (process that reports errors in windows) spawns another process after werfault. If processes such as conhost.exe, cmd.exe or any random application spawns werfault.exe and spawns another process within a very short time after werfault, then it might indicate that the process is trying to gain access to system level priveleges.
